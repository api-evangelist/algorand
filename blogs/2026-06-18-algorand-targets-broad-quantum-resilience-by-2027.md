Algorand is targeting broad quantum resilience by the end of 2027, with initial milestones beginning in Q3 2026 across accounts, consensus, network, and ecosystem layers. The approach includes native Falcon-1024 accounts, hybrid elliptic-curve plus lattice-based Falcon account security, multi-signature enhancements, and hardware wallet integration, with a post-quantum VRF research paper expected in early 2027.
