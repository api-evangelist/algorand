Citing analysis by Google Quantum AI that identifies Algorand among the limited set of smart-contract platforms to achieve real-world deployment of post-quantum cryptography, and Algorand's first PQC-secured transaction in 2025, the Foundation details what it is planning next. The roadmap responds to inquiries from multiple groups, foundations, and researchers about its post-quantum plans.
