Algorand has unveiled a roadmap to achieve quantum resistance by 2027, beginning with upgrades in 2026 that include post-quantum accounts, multisignature wallets, and staking support. The Foundation expects to reach the milestone before NIST retires certain legacy cryptographic standards and three years ahead of a U.S. National Security Agency timeline.
