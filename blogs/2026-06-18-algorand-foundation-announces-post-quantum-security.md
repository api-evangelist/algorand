The Algorand Foundation announced a post-quantum security roadmap committing to broad quantum resilience by the end of 2027, ahead of NIST deprecating legacy RSA key sizes and three years ahead of the NSA's national security systems target. Every protocol layer is addressed, from user wallets and developer tooling to consensus mechanisms.
